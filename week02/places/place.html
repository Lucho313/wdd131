Venezuela

About Venezuela

Venezuela is a country located in northern South America. Its capital is Caracas. It is known for its beautiful landscapes, rich culture, and diverse population.

Culture

Venezuelan culture is a mix of indigenous, African, and Spanish influences. Traditional foods include arepas, cachapas, and pabellón criollo.

Places to Visit

Venezuela has many beautiful places like Margarita Island, Mérida, and the Gran Sabana, which is famous for its mountains and waterfalls.
