BMI Categories

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation that uses your weight and height to estimate your body condition. It is commonly used to classify whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

  • Underweight: less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: 18.5 - 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 - 29.9
  • Obesity: 30 or more
